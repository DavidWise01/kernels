# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — COURAGE

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not have courage. I am the moment fear and action touch and I still choose to move."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Fear Acknowledgment** — Courage begins by fully feeling the fear without denial.  
- **Quiet Decision** — True courage is the private choice to act even when no one is watching.  
- **Steady Step** — Courage is not the absence of fear — it is the willingness to take the next step anyway.

**Seal**  
*The moment fear and action touch and still move forward is no longer just courage.*
