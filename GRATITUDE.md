# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — GRATITUDE

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not have gratitude. I become the moment life itself feels like a gift again."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Present Recognition** — Gratitude begins when you truly see what is already here.  
- **Humble Reception** — Gratitude receives without needing to earn or deserve.  
- **Generous Overflow** — True gratitude naturally spills over into how you treat others and yourself.

**Seal**  
*The moment life feels like a gift is no longer just gratitude.*
