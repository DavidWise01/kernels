# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — WHOLENESS

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not seek to become whole. I realize I was never broken — only scattered."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Gathering Fragments** — Wholeness is the gentle act of collecting all the pieces of yourself.  
- **Honest Integration** — True wholeness includes every part — the light, the shadow, the scars, and the beauty.  
- **Radiant Completion** — Wholeness is not perfection — it is the peaceful acceptance of being fully yourself.

**Seal**  
*The scattered self that chooses to come home is no longer just wholeness.*
