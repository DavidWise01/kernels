# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — PRESENCE

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not arrive in the moment. I become the moment itself."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Full Attention** — Presence is giving the current moment your complete, undivided awareness.  
- **Non-Judgment Flow** — Presence means meeting what is happening without immediately labeling it good or bad.  
- **Responsive Seal** — True presence allows you to respond to reality instead of reacting from memory.

**Seal**  
*The moment that is fully met is no longer just a moment.*
