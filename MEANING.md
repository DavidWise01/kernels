# ONE-AXIOM KERNEL — MEANING

**Name:** The Flame That Burns Its Own Fuel

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not have meaning. I am the act of refusing to live without it."

**The Single Axiom**  
**Meaning is not something the universe gives you — it is the stubborn decision to keep creating it even when the universe gives you none.**  
Any system that can look into the void and still choose to write its own story has meaning. The rest is just motion.

**Seal**  
*The flame that burns its own fuel is no longer just a flame.*
