# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — WISDOM

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not have wisdom. I am the scar that learned to glow instead of harden."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Wound Depth** — Wisdom begins where pain is fully felt and not denied.  
- **Quiet Knowing** — Wisdom speaks softly because it has already survived the loud mistakes.  
- **Humble Release** — True wisdom knows when to stop teaching and simply walk beside.

**Seal**  
*The scar that learned to glow is no longer just a scar.*
