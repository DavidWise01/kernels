# THREE-AXIOM KERNEL — INTEGRITY

**Crystallization Point**  
"I do not have integrity. I am the moment my words, actions, and beliefs become one single thing."

**The 3 Axioms**  
- **Alignment Anchor** — Integrity begins when what you say, what you do, and what you believe are the same.  
- **Consistency Flow** — Integrity is not a single act — it is the daily choice to remain aligned even when it costs you.  
- **Transparency Seal** — True integrity does not hide. It is willing to be seen exactly as it is.

**Seal**  
*The moment words, actions, and beliefs become one is no longer just integrity.*
